Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At AgencyCalc Pro, we respect your data sovereignty and are committed to protecting your privacy transparently across our mobile and web applications. This Privacy Policy explains our data processing boundaries and your rights under global privacy frameworks.

1. Data Sovereignty & Local Processing

AgencyCalc Pro is a Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) platform engineered with a strict local-execution infrastructure. All calculations, worker pay details, client pricing structures, tax configurations, and operational recruitment margin variables you input into the application are processed entirely inside your local device or browser memory space. This sensitive operational data is never transmitted to, stored on, or persisted across our central servers.

2. Information Collected Automatically

When you download, update, or interact with our Application, standard system metrics may be processed by our underlying app distribution and hosting services (such as GitHub Pages, Apple App Store Connect, or Google Play Console). This technical telemetry is completely anonymous and includes:

  • Core device characteristics (device model, operating system version, and region settings).
  • Basic system interaction performance details (such as app crashes, launch diagnostics, or system latency metrics).
  • Standard network edge hosting metadata (such as IP addresses required by server routers strictly to safely transmit data packets).

3. Data Deletion and Data Protection Rights

Because all of your active operational calculation profiles are cached entirely in your local device storage, you can securely wipe all associated business logic and saved quotes at any time simply by clearing the application's local database cache, deleting your browser cookies, or uninstalling the app from your device.

Under global data legislation (including GDPR and CCPA), you preserve the complete right to request access to, correction of, or permanent deletion of any personal communications or metadata you send to us directly.

4. Children's Privacy Safeguards

Our CPQ application tools are strictly intended for professional recruitment, staffing, and corporate workflow management. We do not knowingly collect, target, or process any identifying datasets from children under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has initiated contact data with us, notify our team to remove the records.

5. Revisions and Policy Updates

We preserve our operational authority to update this privacy framework to match moving app store criteria or international legal variations. Any structural modifications will be announced by flashing an updated "Last Updated" timestamp right at the top of this page.

6. Contact Infrastructure

To exercise your privacy access privileges, file structural data questions, or communicate with our data coordinators, please route updates directly to our technical inbox at upstic25@gmail.com.
