Feature Release v2.4.0 May 2026

Core Calculation & Compliance Features Launched

We are excited to highlight the newest operational compliance mechanics deployed directly inside the active layout interface. These advanced tool modules are fully optimized to handle localized validation heuristics:

1. Custom Territory Configurations Establish clean global baseline presets, administrative operational rules, and specific regional defaults directly optimized for different staffing environments.
2. Advanced Reverse Rate Matrix Execute seamless pay-led or charge-led modeling operations. Reverse-calculate strict target billing requirements straight from expected net employee pay variables instantly.
3. Agency Worker Regulations (AWR) Tracking Integrated logic systems designed to monitor the standard 12-week qualifying period thresholds automatically, keeping shift workflows compliant.
4. Automated Employer NI Calculation Real-time processing parameters for modeling statutory Employer National Insurance contributions, relevant tax limits, and payroll load values on-device.
5. NHS Pre-Employment Audit Standard Strict placement checks mapped out for regulated medical staffing ecosystems, confirming candidate validation directly within client proposal parameters.
6. Pro Floor Margin Safeguards Automated interface alerts designed to warn users immediately if a specific calculation falls below corporate margin limits or statutory minimum wage thresholds.
