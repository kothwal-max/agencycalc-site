Account & Data Deletion Hub

In alignment with Apple App Store and Google Play data privacy regulations, AgencyCalc Pro provides a clear process to permanently delete your application user account and purge linked variables.

How to Submit a Deletion Request

To request permanent erasure of your account tracking logs, follow these explicit instructions:

  1. Send an email request from your registered application sign-in email address directly to upstic25@gmail.com.
  2. Ensure your message utilizes the subject header line: "AgencyCalc Pro - Account Deletion Request".
  3. State your active username or account details clearly to confirm identity boundaries.

Want to request data deletion now? Choose what you want removed, then send the pre-filled email request.

Request account and data deletion

You can request permanent deletion of your AgencyCalc Pro account and associated data.

  • Account profile and authentication identifiers
  • Saved proposal calculations and templates
  • Referral, notification preference, role, and activity data

On mobile, the pre-filled email request can open directly in your Mail app. You can also email upstic25@gmail.com from the support page and write what information you want removed.

Some records may be retained only where legally required, such as payment, tax, fraud prevention, or store subscription records. Please send the request from your registered sign-in email address or include that email in your message so we can identify the account.

Data Protection and Retention Scope

  • Purged Data Fields: Executing your request permanently strips account credentials, authentication tokens, and personal workspace metrics from our database systems.
  • Local System Memory: Since AgencyCalc Pro processes variables locally on your device infrastructure to ensure data sovereignty, please manually wipe your phone's local storage data or clear your web browser cache to drop all saved quotes instantly.
  • Retention Timeframes: Active user data profiles are dropped immediately upon confirmation, and background system backup logs are completely overwritten and purged within an automated 30-day rotation matrix.
