Recruitment Margin Tool

AgencyCalc Pro helps recruitment agencies configure statutory payroll assumptions, price assignments accurately, and quote clients with clear cost and margin breakdowns. It brings pay rates, employment costs, statutory deductions, invoice tax treatment, agency margin, and proposal generation into one practical CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) workspace built for international staffing and recruitment teams.

1. Configure

Set up global or local statutory payroll assumptions, business operational rules, regional employment on-costs, and admin-managed system defaults perfectly matched to any territory your agency serves.

2. Price

Model worker pay variables and calculate target bill/charge rates seamlessly using built-in pay-led or charge-led calculations. Keep perfect track of core agency margins and shift modeling data.

3. Quote

Instantly generate quote-ready outputs, proposal documentation workflows, client-facing financial presentations, and structured data exports with automated threshold and margin safety checks.
